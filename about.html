About VANLEE Computer Accessories Sales & IT Training School

Welcome to VANLEE Computer Accessories Sales and IT Training School, your trusted partner in the world of technology and education. Located in the heart of Kampala at Corner Stone Plaza Building, Kafumbe Mukasa Road, Shop Number EF10, we have been serving the community with excellence since our establishment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses with cutting-edge technology solutions and comprehensive IT training. We strive to bridge the digital divide by providing accessible, high-quality computer accessories, repair services, and educational programs that prepare our clients for the challenges and opportunities of the digital age.

What We Offer

Computer Sales & Accessories

We offer a wide range of computer products including laptops from top brands like Dell, HP, Acer, Lenovo, Toshiba, and more. Our inventory includes accessories such as keyboards, mice, monitors, printers, batteries, RAM chips, SSDs, HDDs, and various other tech essentials.

Repair & Maintenance Services

Our certified technicians provide expert repair and maintenance services for all types of electronic devices. From software installation and troubleshooting to hardware repairs, we ensure your devices are running optimally.

IT Training Programs

Through our INTERNET TECHNOLOGY TRAINING SCHOOL, we offer comprehensive courses in various IT fields. Our experienced instructors provide hands-on training to equip you with practical skills for the digital world.

Field Services

We provide on-site services including camera installation, solar system setup, WiFi installation, and other field activities. Our skilled team ensures professional installation and setup for homes and businesses.
